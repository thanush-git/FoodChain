What we do

FoodChain is a platform designed to reduce food waste and ensure excess food from events, restaurants, and households reaches those in need. By leveraging AI and blockchain technology, we create a seamless connection between food donors and nearby shelters, food banks, and charities.

Our AI-powered system matches available food with the closest recipients in real-time, ensuring that donations are used efficiently and distributed while still fresh. Meanwhile, our blockchain technology guarantees full traceability and transparency, so donors and recipients alike can trust the process from start to finish.
